Ypres Belgium, Fall 1914.

The first major battles to take place in the area of Ypres took place in October 1914. However, during the fierce fighting in the autumn of 1914 the German Army was unable to capture Ypres. The French and British forces had denied the German commanders a route across Belgium to the French coastal ports of Calais and Dunkirk.
